The Process

Here's exactly what we do

01

You tell us about your project

Fill out a short form — what you're building, your budget, your timeline, and where you are. Takes about 10 minutes.

02

We write the exact list of work

We write out everything that needs to get done, so every contractor prices the exact same job.

03

We find the right contractors

We pick licensed, insured contractors with a proven track record for projects like yours.

04

They all bid on the same job

Each contractor sends their price for the exact same work. Now you can actually compare.

05

We push for better pricing

We go back to the contractors and negotiate. We catch fees that shouldn't be there and flag anything that looks off.

06

You get a simple comparison

We send you a clear, easy-to-read report — price, timeline, what's included, reviews, and our recommendation.

07

You pick who you want to hire

Choose the contractor you're most comfortable with. No pressure, no obligation. You're in control.
